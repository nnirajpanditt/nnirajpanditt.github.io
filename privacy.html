Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. LifeRemind is built on a "Privacy First" architecture.

Data Collection

LifeRemind does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers. All information entered into the app (habits, moods, schedules) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to support app functionality, advertising, and stability:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information to provide relevant ads. However, your personal tracker and mood data is entirely inaccessible to them.
  • Firebase (Google Analytics): Used to monitor application performance, stability, and to analyze anonymized usage data to help us improve the app. No personal logs, mood entries, or private tracking schedules are ever sent to Firebase.
