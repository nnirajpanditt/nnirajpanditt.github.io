Data Deletion Instructions & Privacy Rights

At LifeRemind, we respect your right to data erasure. Because we do not store your data on any cloud servers, we do not have a database of your habits or logs to delete on our end. You are in complete control of your data.

Option 1: Clear App Storage (Instant Erasure)

To wipe all databases and start fresh without uninstalling the app:

  1. Open your device's Settings app.
  2. Navigate to Apps or App Management.
  3. Find and select LifeRemind.
  4. Tap on Storage & Cache (or Storage).
  5. Tap Clear Storage (or Clear Data) and confirm.

Option 2: App Uninstallation

Uninstalling the application permanently deletes all stored databases. The Android OS automatically destroys the sandbox storage holding the encrypted databases, leaving no trace behind.

Note: Since all data is local, once it is deleted using either of the methods above, it is lost forever. We cannot recover it for you.
