About LifeRemind

LifeRemind was created with a singular focus: Privacy-First Productivity. In today's digital age, even simple utility applications (like timers, checklists, or habit logs) require you to register, log in, and sync your private habits and routines to remote cloud servers where they are analyzed and tracked.

Our Core Pillars:

  • All-In-One Efficiency: Instead of installing five separate apps to track your water, habits, sleep, workouts, and expenses, LifeRemind bundles them all into a single, compact 30MB app package.
  • Offline Autonomy: The app functions entirely on your device, meaning your logs are accessible even on airplanes, remote hikes, or locations without connectivity.
  • Hardware Encryption: All database tables (mood entries, routines, notes, budgets) are stored behind on-device AES-256 encryption keys managed securely by Android's Keystore.
